About Me

Working as a Software Test Engineer for few years, I knew I was interested in programming, and taught myself how to design simple web pages with HTML and CSS.

My interest in web development stemmed from the desire to create websites and/or apps, visually appealing, but provide a top-notch user experience. After taking many online classes and attending meetup groups and events I particularly fell in love with front-end development, but I've enjoyed learning other languages and frameworks too! Because of this, I'm flexible with my preferences and will continue to use/learn whatever tools are best for the tasks at hand. Check out my resume!
